How We Do It?

At StoryHumor, our process is designed to ensure your vision comes to life seamlessly:

Initial Consultation

We start with a comprehensive discussion about your goals, objectives, and target audience, helping us understand what you want to achieve.

Script Development

Our talented writers draft a compelling script that aligns with your messaging, ensuring clarity and engagement.

Storyboarding

We create visual outlines to set the tone and flow of the video, allowing you to visualise the project before production.

Animation and Design

Our skilled animators bring your script and storyboard to life with captivating visuals that enhance the message.

Voiceover Selection

We choose professional voiceover artists to enhance the narrative and bring your script to life with the right tone.

Final Review and Edits
