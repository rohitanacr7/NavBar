Who we are?

We are a boutique design and animation studio, specializes in crafting video content for a diverse range of clients, including agencies, studios, and production houses operating within the advertising and entertainment sectors.

In 2020, the way we work, transformed, With remote work taking places, We started as small team to deliver excellence to studios and agencies. Our driving force is the satisfaction of our clients, motivating us to exceed expectations. Beyond work, we savor burgers at our cherished café, nurturing our camaraderie

To initiate the process, reach out and share your requirements. Upon confirming compatibility, we'll present a detailed proposal showcasing our capabilities. Once approved, we'll send a Video Services Agreement along with an initial invoice accounting for 50% of the total project cost. Additionally, we'll furnish a creative brief for your input and schedule our kick-off call.

The pricing is determined by several factors such as video duration, style, and intricacy. For instance, a tailored 2D animated video generally falls within the range of $1,000 to $5,000. Interested in a series of videos? Fantastic! We're open to exploring package pricing options to streamline expenses. Nevertheless, don't be daunted by the figures. Reach out to us, share details about your project, and we'll provide clarity on the expenses involved.

As audience attention spans remain limited, we aim to craft concise videos, typically spanning 30 to 120 seconds. Despite this brevity, we ensure essential information is conveyed. While we might not cover every marketing detail, our videos are designed to intrigue viewers and prompt them to consider the next steps, offering just enough information to captivate interest.
